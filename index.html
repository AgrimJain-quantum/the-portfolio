Who I Am

I am a photographer and video editor who enjoys capturing stories through visuals. What started as a curiosity for cameras and editing gradually became a way of observing the world more closely—finding interesting details, emotions, and moments that often go unnoticed.

Through photography, I explore perspectives, light, composition, and the unique character of places and people. Through video editing, I bring those moments together to create engaging narratives that connect with audiences and leave a lasting impression. Whether it's documenting an event, creating cinematic visuals, or experimenting with new editing styles, I am always looking for ways to improve my craft and tell better stories.

My creative work is driven by a simple belief: every frame should have a purpose, and every project should communicate something meaningful. I enjoy blending technical precision with artistic expression to create content that is both visually appealing and impactful.

Outside of creative projects, my background in engineering influences how I approach visual storytelling—combining creativity with structure, attention to detail, and continuous learning.
